Welcome to Calc JS


Calc JS is an online JavaScript calculator which allows you to do arithmetical and non-arithmetical operations using your web browser. It's main goal is to provide an accessible, centralized, simplified and open source virtual space for your daily mathematical operations.
With a few clicks, you can sum, substract, multiply, divide, obtaining the pi number, power of a number and other stuffs in an easy way.
